People
Who are we:

Owing to the free education system in Sri Lanka, brought together in 1985 to the Faculty of Engineering,
University of Peradeniya, from various part of the country,
E 85 Pera group is a conglomeration of Engineers. Having obtained a basic degree in Engineering in various disciplines
many of them are now renowned academics, business magnates, high officials in both public and private sector and much sought and highly respected specialists.
Further, they are spread all over the world making the group a truly global collection of individuals.

Having embarked on their lives journey over three decades ago they were able to maintain an unwavering and
undiminished sense of friendship and care for each other. On various occasions they have voluntarily and wholeheartedly come forward
to help their fellow batch mates in times of distress and need, transcending all man made barriers such as race, religion, language, social stature etc,.



Mission
Collective Vision

Continue with the camaraderie built during the university days and work as a team in sharing financial
and intellectual resources in helping each other and the society at large through collective projects.
